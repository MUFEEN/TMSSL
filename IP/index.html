TRADITIONAL MEDICAL TREATMENTS IN SRI LANKA

Sri Lanka has its own indigenous system of traditional medicine which has been practiced for over five thousand years.
Sri Lanka's indigenous system of traditional medicine is known as Desheeya Chikitsa and has been influenced by
Ayurveda, Siddha of India and Unani medicine of Greece.
Using this website you can find the details about the traditional medical tretaments and other related details.
